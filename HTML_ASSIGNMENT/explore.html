Bonifacio

Located on a limestone cliff at the southern end of Corsica, Bonificaio offers one of the most spectacular views in Europe with its rich history and landscapes. The town is divided into two areas: Vieille Ville perched on a cliff that overlooks the Tyrrhenian sea, with ancient churches, old town and a 9th century citadel. At the bottom of the cliff lies a llively harbour with busy ports, yachts and markets, the entire city framed by beautiful medieval architecture that transports the visitors back in time.

Rhine Waterfalls

The Largest waterfall in Europe spanning spanning over 150m wide, located in Schaffhausen surrounded by lush green forest, and a medival castle perched upon the surrounding Cliffs. In the middle of the waterfall lies ancient rock formations carved through the rushing water on which one can climb observing the spectacular view of the misty cascading water and the serene water basin below where boats sail- all set against the backfrop of Europe’s most unique landscape.

Svartisen Glaciers

Located in Nordland, Norwayy, Svartisen consists of two majestic glaciers namely Vestisen to the west and Østisen to the east. The name Svartisen in Norwegian translates to ‘Black Ice’ referring to the dark blue hue of glacier, surrounded by rugged mountains, lakes and other glaciers. Svartisen reigning the 6th largest glacier in Europe offers a surreal landscape of the ice shimmering down the green valley sitting near Glomåga river creating the most unforgettable view.

Faroe Islands

Formed from volcanic eruptions, Faroe island consists of 18 islands between Iceland,UK and norway in the North Atlantic Ocean. These islands are around 30-40m above sea level with steep cliffs with river and waterfalls cascading into the ocean creating an optical illusion where land and sea seem to merge. Adding to the scenery Faroe islands is known as the land of grass roof tops and colorful village surrounded by lush green landscape creating a timeless scenery of Nordic beauty.

Bled, Slovenia

Bled located in Slovenia is known for its island resting in the hear of a glacial lake surrounded by mountains, forests and a castle situated on a cliff overlooking the water. At the center of the island lies a 17th century church. This scenery along with the misty mountaintops and forest creates the most magical and fairy tale like landscape in all of Europe.
