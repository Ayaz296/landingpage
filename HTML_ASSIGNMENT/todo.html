To-do List

Drawn from several traveler reviews, trusted travel guides and blogs we have compiled a list of must-see attractions, experiences and activities when visiting these fabulous destinations so that you don’t miss the highlights that make these landscapes truly unforgettable.

Bonifacio

Places to visit:
  • Citadel
  • King Aragon’s Stairway (Escalier du Roi d’Aragon)
  • Iles Lavezzi
  • Explore Vieille Ville
Activities:
  1. Swimming
  2. Yacht tour
  3. Hiking King Aragon’s Stairway

Rhine Waterfalls

Places to visit:
  • Laufen Castle
  • Miniature World near Rhine Falls
  • View the waterfalls from a platform or the rocks
  • Lindt Home of Chocolate (54 km away)
Activities:
  1. Boating
  2. Dining at Restaurant Schloss Laufen

Svartisen Glaciers

Places to visit:
  • Walk around Svartisvatnet Lake
  • Hike at Svartisen Glacier
  • View waterfalls from platforms or rocks
  • Village of Holandsfjord
Activities:
  1. Saltfjellet–Svartisen National Park
  2. Wildlife spotting
  3. Cave exploration

Faroe Islands

Places to visit:
  • Fossa Waterfall
  • Gásadalur Village
  • View the ocean from the sea cliffs
Activities:
  1. Exploring the islands
  2. Hiking across valleys and mountains
  3. Trying dried mutton and fish in restaurants

Bled, Slovenia

Places to visit:
  • Bled Castle
  • Church of the Assumption of Mary
  • Vintgar Gorge
Activities:
  1. Fishing
  2. Canoeing and kayaking
  3. Summer tobogganing
